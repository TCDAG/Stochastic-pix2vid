Lotter, W., Kreiman, G., & Cox, D. (2017). Deep predictive coding networks for video prediction and unsupervised learning. In International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR). This paper presents a deep multi-scale architecture for video prediction that captures both short-term and long-term dependencies in video sequences.
